# Code of conduct

This project expects respectful, focused collaboration.

## Standards

Participants should:

- keep discussion technical and evidence-based;
- respect responsible-use boundaries for security research;
- avoid harassment, personal attacks, and discriminatory language;
- keep private vulnerabilities out of public issues;
- avoid pressuring maintainers to ship unsafe or overclaimed features.

## Enforcement

The maintainer may edit, hide, or remove comments; close issues; reject contributions; or
block participants who repeatedly violate these expectations. Security-sensitive reports
should follow [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md), not public discussion threads.
